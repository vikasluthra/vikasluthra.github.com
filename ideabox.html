Ideabox

A web paltform for idea development,sharing and review
Duration:Sept 2012-Nov 2012

About the Project

In the Semester project for Interaction Design, we designed a system for idea sharing and development within the entrepreneur community at IITG. With the problem statement of not having that many ideas realized to a good level in the Entrepreneurship cell. The platform would allow idea sharing, expert feedbacks and development for the budding entrepreneurs with incorporation of creative tools and design methodology. The different stages of idea development were studied and analyzed. User interviews were conducted and a few case studies of successful ideas were studied as a part of the research The proposed system would have a host website and motivated users to provide feedbacks and suggesting techniques of further development. Also, the system has many creative problem solving tools and ‘Workspace’ for the user’s projects, providing them a dynamic environment to work on the idea
