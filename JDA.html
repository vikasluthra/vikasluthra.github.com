Supply Chain Management-JDA

Dashboard for Master Planners
Project Guide:Prof.(Dr.) Pradeep Yammiyawar,Head of CET,IIT guwahati
Duration:Feb 2013-Apr 2013
Industrial-Academia Project
Industrial Client:JDA
Keywords:Supply chain planning,dashboard,interaction design


Brief Description

The project was carried out in collaboration with JDA Software, a leader in global supply chain management. The aim of the project was to create a collaborative dashboard for master planners in the supply chain industry to aid them in efficient decision making and planning. A user-centered design approach was followed. Literature and online research was done. Based on the user interviews, personas and use-cases sent by the client, insights were drawn through affinity analysis, storyboards, task flows etc. with respect to the roles, responsibilities and tasks of master planners. These insights were further used to showcase role specific data i.e. performance based indexes (KPI’s), trends, alerts/warnings, deadlines, etc. on a platform that could seamlessly integrated on desktops and other portable devices. Wireframes and Mockups were created for desktops and presented to the client
