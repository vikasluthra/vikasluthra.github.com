Microsoft Research

Rich Interactive Narratives
Done as Part of Two Day Workshop By Microsoft Research
Duration:Aug 2012

Given Brief

To come up with an idea or storyboard for a "Rich Interactive Narrative" which could show application of interactive content videos in different areas .This was floated as a competition by Microsoft Research and needs to build using MSR RIN Studio . We also got to attend the two day workshop by Microsoft Research regarding this.


Solution

After a little bit research about the problem and technology given we have brain writing session in which we tried to come out with several ideas relating to different areas and fields .Some of the ideas that we came out with included-Interactive books, Interactive cricket match highlights,a documentary on evolution of Indian music etc. Finally we decided to work on the concept - "Interactive E-paper reading" ,making it as a proof of concept


Proposed Concept

Currently E-paper have a static experience in which user read the text and see static images. So we thought of converting this experience into an interactive ,dynamic and more functional with help RIN Studio(a software for building interactive videos or narratives). Some of the possible key features of this E-paper are-
→Narration having the important headlines from the paper but viewer could also explore other articles in paper
→User could listen the text of any article on the page.
→They could also see the collection of images.videos,full match scorecards,movie trailers etc related to different articles.
→In future we could also use it to book our movie tickets,have interactive scorecards , sensex meter etc through this E-paper.
→Everyday an interactive video containing all the news of previous day would be uploaded online from where we all can enjoy it.The Interactive paper could also be new mode of news for surface users
This idea was presented as proof of concept narrative to MSR
